ScentSwap ‚Äî Product Specification & README
The Vision
ScentSwap is Australia's first AI-powered fragrance bartering marketplace. No buying, no selling ‚Äî just swapping. Trade that impulse buy sitting on your shelf for something you'll actually wear.
The fragrance community has a problem: people accumulate bottles they don't love, decants they've finished exploring, and blind buys that didn't work out. Selling is awkward, prices are disputed, and scammers lurk everywhere.
ScentSwap removes money from the equation entirely. Pure trade. AI-assisted matching. Verified users only. Fair swaps guaranteed.

Core Philosophy
1. No Money, No Drama ‚Äî Swapping eliminates price haggling and payment scams
2. AI as the Neutral Party ‚Äî The AI mediates fairness, suggests matches, verifies authenticity
3. Trust is Everything ‚Äî Verification, ratings, and swap history build community trust
4. Australian First ‚Äî Starting local means accountable users and reasonable shipping

How It Works ‚Äî User Journey
1. Join & Verify
* Sign up with email
* Verify Australian identity (drivers licence or Medicare card via ID verification API)
* Confirm Australian address (for shipping)
* Optional: link social profiles for additional trust signals
* Receive "Verified Swapper" badge
2. Build Your Swap Cabinet
* Add fragrances you want to swap
* For each bottle:
    * Snap photos (AI analyses for authenticity markers)
    * Select fragrance from database (auto-linked to Fragrantica)
    * Specify: size, fill level (%), batch code, condition
    * Add personal notes ("This one's a beast mode performer")
* AI auto-generates scent description, notes pyramid, and comparable fragrances
3. Set Your Swap Preferences
* For each listing, specify what you'd accept:
    * Specific fragrances you're hunting
    * Or broader preferences: designer/niche, gender, scent family, season
    * Swap ratio flexibility: 1:1, 1:2, 1:3 (based on relative value)
    * Willing to add decants to balance a trade?
4. Discover & Match
* Browse the marketplace
* AI suggests matches based on:
    * Your wishlist vs. their cabinet
    * Scent profile compatibility
    * Fair value matching
    * Geographic proximity (faster shipping)
* "Swap Match" notifications when mutual interest detected
5. Propose a Swap
* Select your bottle(s) ‚Üí Select their bottle(s)
* AI Fairness Check runs automatically
    * Compares market values, fill levels, rarity
    * Suggests adjustments if unbalanced
    * Shows confidence score: "This swap is 94% fair"
* Add a message, submit proposal
6. Negotiate (AI-Mediated)
* Chat with the other swapper
* AI Mediator available:
    * "Is this a fair trade?" ‚Üí AI gives neutral assessment
    * "What could I add to make this work?" ‚Üí AI suggests from your cabinet
    * Dispute? AI reviews and suggests resolution
* Both parties must confirm final swap terms
7. Lock & Ship
* Both confirm ‚Üí Swap is LOCKED
* Shipping labels generated (or manual shipping with tracking required)
* Packing guidelines displayed:
    * Wrap bottle in bubble wrap
    * Seal cap with tape
    * Photo of packed item required before shipping
    * Upload tracking number
* Status updates: Packed ‚Üí Shipped ‚Üí In Transit ‚Üí Delivered
8. Confirm & Rate
* Both parties confirm receipt
* Rate the experience (1-5 stars):
    * Accuracy (was it as described?)
    * Packaging (was it safe?)
    * Communication (were they responsive?)
    * Overall swap experience
* Written review optional
* Swap added to both users' history

The Swap Fairness Algorithm
Not all bottles are equal. A 10ml decant isn't worth a 100ml full presentation. The AI calculates:
Factors:
* Current market value (scraped from Fragrantica, Facebook groups, auction sites)
* Bottle size
* Fill level percentage
* Condition (new/like new/good/fair)
* Rarity/discontinuation status
* Batch desirability (some batches more sought after)
* Age (vintage value vs. degradation)
Output:
* Swap Value Score for each item
* Comparison showing if trade is balanced
* Suggestions to balance if not: "Add a 10ml decant of X to make this fair"
User Override:
* Users can accept "unfair" swaps knowingly
* System flags but doesn't block
* "I know this isn't equal value but I really want this bottle" ‚Äî fine, proceed

Shipping & Handling
Mandatory Packing Protocol:
1. Secure the spray nozzle (tape or remove)
2. Wrap bottle in bubble wrap (minimum 2 layers)
3. Place in rigid box with padding (no movement when shaken)
4. Seal outer box securely
5. Photograph packed item before sealing
6. Upload photo to swap record
Shipping Requirements:
* Tracking number mandatory
* Signature on delivery recommended for high-value
* Insurance recommended (user's responsibility)
* Australia Post, Sendle, or CouriersPlease accepted
* Interstate shipping: 3-7 business days expected
* Upload tracking within 48 hours of swap lock or auto-cancel
Shipping Issues:
* Delayed? System auto-extends confirmation window
* Lost? Dispute process triggered, both users' ratings protected
* Damaged? Photo evidence required, AI assesses fault, resolution process
